Who We Are

Our Company

Camcall is a forward-thinking Indian brand that unites retail excellence, technological innovation, and FMCG distribution under one umbrella. With a strong foundation across multiple business domains, we aim to make everyday life simpler, smarter, and more connected.

We operate through three dynamic divisions:

Camcall Retail

Our retail division focuses on consumer products, sold through our Julebi app & website as well as leading e-commerce platforms.

Camcall Technologies

Our technology division specializes in app development and manages digital platforms like the OldSyllabus service platform.

Camcall FMCG

Our FMCG division delivers quality fast-moving consumer goods that meet everyday needs with reliability and value.

In the past, Camcall Technologies Also explored iOT and surveillance systems manufacturing as part of its innovation journey, experiences that helped shape our current approach to technology solutions.

Our Team

Behind Camcall is a passionate team of entrepreneurs, developers, retail experts, and innovators dedicated to solving real-world problems with creativity and determination. Our team combines:

  • Tech Innovators: Building mobile & web applications and managing service-based platforms.
  • FMCG Specialists: Ensuring quality distribution of fast-moving consumer goods across markets.

Together, we combine business expertise with technological vision to deliver impactful solutions that serve both today's needs and tomorrow's possibilities.

Our Journey

From Vision to Reality

Camcall began with a simple yet powerful idea: bridging the gap between retail, technology, and FMCG under one cohesive brand. We recognized that the future of business would depend on seamlessly integrating physical products with digital experiences.

Founded in 2016, we established Camcall Retail Private Limited, entering the retail space with carefully selected consumer products that meet the needs of modern Indian households. Our approach was different from the start - we focused on quality, affordability, and accessibility through digital channels.

As we grew, we expanded into technology with Camcall Technologies and later established our FMCG division to serve the everyday needs of consumers with quality products.
